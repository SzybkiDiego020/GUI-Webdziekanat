Dear Students,

From the 19th of March 2011, there is no need to manually register a student in the WebDziekanat system.

Access to the portal is possible with a password obtained at the WIKAMP Centrum Customer Service Office (Building B22 - TUL Library, 2nd floor, room 203). In the event of problems with access to the WebDziekanat system, using the correct password to log in to the University's e-mail, report the errors or other problems to the Dean's Office that is appropriate to your field of study.
